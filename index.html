Digital Guide

The Complete Bike Mechanics Handbook

The Complete Bike Mechanics Handbook is a 100-page digital guide built for cyclists who want to understand and maintain their own road or gravel bike without guessing. Written by WeeeCycle, a dedicated road and gravel service shop in Lexington, KY, every chapter is grounded in real workshop practice.

menu_book What's Inside:

  • 13 chapters covering every core system — chain, derailleurs, cassette, brakes, wheels, tires, cockpit, fit, and seasonal storage
  • Dedicated road vs. gravel comparisons throughout — 1× drivetrain logic, GRX vs. SRAM XPLR, 700c vs. 650b wheels, tubeless tyre pressure formulas, flat-mount disc brake specifics
  • Torque specifications for every critical fastener, so you never guess on a carbon component
  • Step-by-step procedures: hydraulic brake bleeds, tubeless conversion, wheel truing, derailleur indexing, clutch derailleur service, dropper post maintenance
  • AI-illustrated technical diagrams in every chapter — labeled anatomy, exploded views, system cross-sections
  • Pro tips, warning callouts, and maintenance calendars built from shop experience

Whether you're wrenching on a Shimano 105 road bike or dialing in a GRX gravel build with a 10-51T cassette, this handbook gives you the knowledge to do it right the first time.
